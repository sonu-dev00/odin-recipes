balsamic chicken and mushrooms

balsamic chicken and mushrooms has bold balsamic flavor, balanced with honey and mustard. The chicken is moist, and tender, with succulent mushrooms bathed in zippy sauce.

Ingrediants

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 (8 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts, halved horizontally
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, cleaned and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup reduced sodium chicken broth
  • 1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme, plus more for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium heat
  2. season chicken with 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Add chicken to skillet. Cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and lightly browned, turning once halfway through , 6 minutes. Remove chicken from skillt; keep warm.
  3. Add muschrooms to skillet. Cook until mushrooms are browned and their juices have evaporated, about 4 minutes.

  5. Return chicken to skillet, cover and heat through until a thermometer inserted into the thickest portion registers 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), 3 to 4 minutes.
  6. Garnish with additional fresh thyme.
